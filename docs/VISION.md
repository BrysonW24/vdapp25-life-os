# Life OS — Vision & Core Concept

> *"If I'm alive, then why wouldn't I want to know myself better than anyone else?"*

---

## What This Is

This is not a productivity app.
This is not a finance app.
This is not a habit tracker.

**This is a Personal Intelligence that evolves with you.**

A **Performance Operating System for High-Agency Individuals** — a digital coach, a mirror, a conscience.

The goal: to help you extract your full potential before you die.

Modern adult life removes the structure that sport and academia provided.

In sport:
- There's feedback

- There's standards
- There's accountability
- There's competition
- There's game tape

Most people leave structured environments and **decay quietly.**

Most apps respond to this with trackers, dashboards, and pie charts.

That's not enough.

## Core Architecture: Four Layers

---

### Layer 1 — Identity Declaration

The system forces declaration of self. No vagueness.

You define:
- **Vision statement** — what you're building toward
- **Life view** — how you see the world and your place in it
- **Work view** — why you work and what it means
- **5 Pillars** — the non-negotiable domains of your life
- **Core values** — your operating principles
- **Long-term mission** — the 10–20 year arc

Then, per pillar, you declare **numeric standards**:

```
Health:
  - 4 workouts/week
  - Resting HR < 50
  - VO2 Max target: X

Finance:
  - 40% savings rate
  - $X net worth by year Y

Learning:
  - 12 books/year
  - 1 technical deep dive/quarter

Business:
  - Revenue targets
  - Output metrics

Relationships:
  - Time investment standards
  - Quality indicators
```

This is your **constitution**. Everything else is measured against it.

---

### Layer 2 — Execution Layer

Your daily behavior, captured and structured:

- **Goals** — long-term broken into sprints
- **Sprints** — time-boxed focus periods
- **Tasks** — actionable work units
- **Habits** — recurring behaviors with streak tracking
- **Calendar** — execution view overlaid with goals
- **Reflection cycles** — AM/PM prompts, weekly and monthly reviews

This is your "game tape."

---

### Layer 3 — Intelligence Layer

The data engine. Objective signals pulled from:

- Calendar (time allocation)
- Finance tracker (spending, saving, net worth)
- Habit tracker (consistency, streaks)
- Health metrics (sleep, workouts, energy)
- Project output (deliverables, milestones)
- Learning logs (books, courses, skills acquired)

The system has ground truth. You cannot hide from it.

---

### Layer 4 — Advisory Layer (The Most Important)

This is where the system becomes what you're imagining.

It constantly:
- Compares **identity** vs **behavior**
- Detects inconsistency
- Surfaces opportunities
- Alerts on misalignment
- Suggests concrete actions
- **Challenges rationalizations**

Example challenge:
> *"You've consumed 14 hours of passive media this week.
> You declared mastery of AI as a 3-year objective.
> Is this aligned?"*

That's not a productivity app. **That's a mirror.**

Another example:
> *"You said health is a top 3 pillar.
> You've skipped workouts 5 times this month.
> Is this aligned with your vision?"*

The Gap Engine classifies your state continuously:
- **Aligned** — declared standards met
- **Drifting** — gradual degradation
- **Avoiding** — consistent gap in one domain
- **Improving** — trajectory positive
- **Regressing** — trajectory negative across multiple domains

Example:

> Your savings surplus is $X. Your calendar has a 3-week window in March.
> Flight deals to Rome are at a 10-year low.
> Do you want to prototype this life path?"*

That's not budgeting.
That's **Life Design** with real-time context.

## Personality & Calibration

The system adapts to the person.

Calibration inputs:
- **Personality type** (16 Personalities / MBTI — e.g., INTJ Architect)
- **Tone preference** — rational/clinical, stoic/disciplined, athletic/competitive, philosophical/reflective, or a blend
- **Current state** — high-energy vs recovering, building vs consolidating
- **Goal orientation** — wealth building, health, creativity, impact, legacy

The coaching voice changes with your state of mind. It knows when to push and when to support. It calibrates messaging based on your values, mission, and purpose.

For an INTJ: concise, strategic, systems-thinking, forward-looking.
For an ENFP: contextual, story-driven, possibility-oriented.

## Visual Language

This is not a cheap productivity app. The aesthetic must match the ambition.

**The standard:**
`Apple × Bloomberg × Notion × Wealth Command Center`

- Dark mode default
- Minimal clutter
- Elegant, purposeful animations
- High-contrast typography
- Premium feel — no neon crypto aesthetics

Standout visuals:
1. **Freedom Meter** — circular gauge filling as passive income approaches lifestyle cost. Glows gold when you reach it.
2. **Money as Particles** — every dollar a particle, flowing into assets (multiply), lifestyle (disappear), or debt (leak). Toggle: "what if 10% redirected to ETF?" Watch particles move.
3. **Future Self Projection** — two avatars side by side: Current You and Future You, changing based on savings rate, health consistency, habit streaks, debt reduction
4. **Habit–Wealth Correlation Graph** — workout streak vs productivity, study streak vs income, spending discipline vs net worth growth
5. **Spending DNA** — visual fingerprint of your category proportions, volatility, savings ratio, risk tolerance across years
6. **Goal Timeline Bar** — shrinks dynamically as you increase monthly surplus

## Technical Architecture (Initial)

**Frontend:** React 18 + Vite + TypeScript
**Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4, dark mode default
**Storage:** Dexie.js (IndexedDB) — fully offline, no cloud dependency initially
**Charts:** Recharts + custom canvas animations
**AI Layer:** Claude API — advisory, challenge engine, opportunity detection
**Personality Engine:** 16 Personalities / MBTI calibration on onboarding

**Core Modules:**

| Module | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| Identity Setup | Vision builder, life view / work view writer, values |
| Freedom Dashboard | Passive income meter, financial runway, particle visual |
| Goal Engine | Long-term → Sprint model, timeline shrink simulation |
| Habit Engine | Calendar, streak correlation, execution graph |
| Gap Engine | Declared vs observed, alignment classification |
| Challenge Engine | Advisory layer, contradiction surfacing |
| Reflection Engine | Monthly migration, AM/PM prompts, AI summarization |
| Resource Library | Books, concepts, contextual suggestions |
| Opportunity Engine | Real-time context matching, life path prototyping |
